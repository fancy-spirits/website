Welcome to Fancy Spirits, an independent music label founded by a passionate artist collective. Our mission is to create a community that is dedicated to the field of electronic music, embracing all genres within it. At Fancy Spirits, we work closely with our artists, helping them to bring their music onto streaming services and get heard. Our artists are free to express themselves in any way they like, and we believe that this freedom is essential for creating truly unique and authentic music. Join us in our journey to discover new and exciting talent in the vast world of electronic music.


<br/>

All of us make electronic music. A variety from heavy bass music to lofi and chill pop music. Oftentimes genres like Drum & Bass, Dubstep, House, Electro and subgenres of those. Everyone has a unique style and approach to their music making and supports each other.
